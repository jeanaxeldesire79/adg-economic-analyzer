Data Sources

All data sourced from the World Bank World Development Indicators (WDI) database, accessed via the ADG ECOWAS Analytics pipeline. 88 indicators across 15 West African countries, covering GDP, population, health, education, energy, trade, and more.

Primary Source: World Bank Group. "World Development Indicators." data.worldbank.org. Accessed via ADG ECOWAS_Analysis dataset.

Indicator Categories

 Economic: GDP, inflation, Gini, FDI, trade, savings, revenue

 Demographic: Population, fertility, density, labor force

 Health: Life expectancy, mortality, access to electricity

 Education: Literacy rate, education expenditure

 Energy & Environment: Energy use, renewables, CO₂, forest, PM2.5

 Infrastructure: Internet, mobile, fixed telephone subscriptions

References: World Bank (2024). World Development Indicators. Washington, D.C.: World Bank Group. DOI: 10.57966/9H8T-2B42.

Limitations

Historical data for some countries (Liberia, Sierra Leone) has gaps during conflict periods. Data quality varies by indicator and reporting year.
